Timestamp Microservice

Example Usage:

  • Get timestamp from date string:
    fcc-backend-project1.plcoster.repl.co/api/2015-12-25
  • Get timestamp from Unix timestamp:
    fcc-backend-project1.plcoster.repl.co/api/1451001600000
  • Get current timestamp:
    fcc-backend-project1.plcoster.repl.co/api/

Example Output:

{"unix":1451001600000, "utc":"Fri, 25 Dec 2015 00:00:00 GMT"}
